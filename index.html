My research focuses on how human culture and cognition interact in language evolution and development, using a multimodal approach that accounts for the role of the body in language production and comprehension. I use both experimental and naturalistic data to investigate the role of multimodal iconicity in language learning in both children and adults. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Centre for Language Evolution at the University of Edinburgh.
